Illegal Forward Pass, Flag Guard End Zone

authored by Jake Harvanchik

Third and goal onB's 10. PasserA-1rolls out to his right and throws the ball atB's 9which is intercepted byB-5in his own end zone.B-5swats away the hand ofA-2and exits the end zone to eventually get deflagged atB's 3. What are ways this can be enforced?

A-1committed an illegal forward pass penalty atA's 9.B-5then committed a flag guarding penalty in his own end zone.
IfTeam Bdeclinesthe illegal forward pass penalty byTeam A, thenTeam Bhas committed a safety due to the flag guarding penalty occurring in their own end zone. Two points are awarded toTeam A, and it'sTeam A's ball atA's 30.
IfTeam Bacceptsthe illegal forward pass penalty andTeam Aacceptsthe flag guarding penalty, then it'sTeam A's ball, replay down. This is a double foul (Rule 10-3-3C), so the penaltiesoffset.
