Roughing the Passer Enforcement

authored by Jake Harvanchik

Fourth and 8 onA's 32. PasserA-1is roughed byB-1:(a)forward pass is complete toA-2who is deflagged atA's 35;(b)pass is incomplete;(c)forward pass is complete toA-2who is deflagged atA's 28. What is the result?

Roughing the passer. In(a),A'sball, 1st and 15 onB's 35; in(b)and(c), it isA'sball, 1st and 18 onB's 38. Enforce from the previous spot. In(a), enforce from the end of the run.
A roughing the passer foul is an exception to the All-But-One Principle (10-2-2).
